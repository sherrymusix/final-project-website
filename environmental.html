Water Purification System Model

This project demonstrates a simple and effective water purification process using layers of gravel, sand, charcoal, and cotton. The model explains how impurities, suspended particles, and harmful substances are removed from contaminated water.

Rainwater Harvesting System

This project shows how rainwater can be collected from rooftops and stored for future use. The model explains water conservation techniques and their role in reducing water scarcity. It demonstrates channels, storage tanks, and filtration units, promoting sustainable water management in urban areas.

Solid Waste Management System

Greenhouse Effect Model

This project demonstrates how greenhouse gases trap heat in the Earth’s atmosphere. Using a closed transparent setup, the model shows temperature rise due to heat retention. It explains global warming, climate change, and the impact of human activities on Earth’s climate.

    Team: Climate Awareness Group

Deforestation Impact Model

This project highlights the environmental impact of deforestation on ecosystems. The model compares areas with dense forests and deforested land, showing effects on soil erosion, wildlife, and climate. It promotes forest conservation and sustainable land use.
