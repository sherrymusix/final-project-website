Food Chain and Ecosystem Balance Model

This project represents the flow of energy in an ecosystem through a food chain.It shows how plants and animals depend on each other for survival.The model includes: Producers (plants), Primary consumers (herbivores), Secondary consumers (carnivores), Decomposers. Plant and Animal Cell Structure Model

This project explains the internal structure of plant and animal cells. It shows different organelles and their functions inside the cell. The model includes: Nucleus, Cytoplasm, Mitochondria, Ribosomes, Cell membrane, Cell wall (plant cell), Different colors are used to clearly distinguish each part. The project helps students understand how cells are the basic unit of life.

Human Respiratory System – Working Model

This project explains the structure and functioning of the human respiratory system. It demonstrates how air enters the lungs, how oxygen is absorbed, and how carbon dioxide is released during breathing. The model includes major parts such as: Nose and nasal cavity, Trachea (windpipe), Bronchi and bronchioles, Lungs and alveoli. A simple balloon mechanism is used to show inhalation and exhalation. This project helps students understand gas exchange and the importance of healthy lungs.
